About Me

My career in technology started when I was 6 and my parents brought home a computer they where given at work. Manufactured by Sperry Corperation, this was an 8086 that did not even come with an operating system, only a basic compiler on a 5.25 floppy disk. Over the next 10 years I found a passion for online connectivity through BBS's. Making lifelong friendships, and solidifying a foundation of learning that has carried me through my 24+ year career in technology.

Given that my livelyhood is highly technical, my outside intersts are not. I have wide variety of passions and interests. I love to cook, and I have a professional scuba diver certification. More recently I have been studying blacksmithing as a hobby.

I have a passion for learning and hold multiple degrees. I have degrees and certifications in Electronics, Technical Management, Culinary Arts, Scuba Diving, Project Mangement, and an MBA.
